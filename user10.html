Half-Life

During a failed experiment at the Black Mesa Research Facility, an explosion opens a portal to an alien world. Physicist Gordon Freeman survives, but must now battle both aliens and government forces to save humanity from an invasion of Earth.

Valve's first title, Half-Life, combines action and adventure with award-winning technology to deliver a thrillingly immersive world where players must think to survive. The game features an innovative combination of combat, puzzle-solving and storytelling.
